# System Overview & Domain Scope

## Selected Domain: Automated Product Sorting and Packaging System (Factory Conveyor Belt)

## Reasons for Selection:

1. **Practical Application of the Mod-10 Counter:**  
In industrial packaging lines, products are commonly grouped into batches of 10 items (for example: 10 water bottles, 10 milk cartons) or packed into boxes containing 10 units. The synchronous Mod-10 counter plays a core role in accurately counting from 0 to 9. When the counter reaches the 10th state (transitioning from state 9 back to 0), the system generates a trigger signal to perform actions such as pushing a product batch or sealing a package automatically.

2. **Strict Requirement for the Self-starting Mechanism:**  
In factory environments, industrial noise generated by motors, inverters, and high-power equipment can easily alter Flip-Flop states and cause invalid states greater than 9. If the circuit becomes stuck or stops operating, the conveyor system may halt, leading to production downtime and financial losses. The self-starting mechanism enables the system to automatically recover to a valid state within a single clock pulse, ensuring continuous and stable operation without requiring a manual reset.
