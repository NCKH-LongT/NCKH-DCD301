```
"decoder based" AND "combinational logic"
"decoder" AND "look-up table" AND "LUT"
"decoder" AND "ECC" AND "glitch power"
"hierarchical decoder" AND "synthesis"
"decoder" AND "post-quantum" AND "hardware"
```
